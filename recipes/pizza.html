Not Your Papa's Pizza Recipe

What You're Making

If you ask ten people what their favorite food is, I bet eleven of those people say pizza.

Pizza is one of the most fun and easiest homemade dishes to prepare. Everyone from grandchild to grandma can get involved.

What You'll Need

  • For the Sauce

    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1/4 (12 oz can) tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

  • For the Crust

    • 3 1/4 cups all purpose flour, or more as needed
    • 2 (.25 ounce) envelopes pizza crust yeast
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1 1/2 cups very warm water (120 degrees F to 130 degrees F)
    • 1/2 cup oil

  • Toppings

    • 1 cup shredded mozarella cheese, or more to taste
    • 1 (6 ounce) package pepperoni

      • (Pssssssttttt, Feel free to add any other toppings you have a craving for, be creative!)
