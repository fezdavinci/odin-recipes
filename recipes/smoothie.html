Smackin' Smoothie Recipe

What You're Making

Nothing says quick breakfast or summer pick me up like a refreshing frozen beverage, and best of all you can always make them at home.

Today I'll be showing you one of my favorite smoothie recipes. Don't let the short list and fast prep fool you, this baby packs a punch!

What You'll Need

  1. Frozen strawberries:Or you can sub in any othe rkind of frozen berries that you desire.
  2. Banana:I usually prefer to use fresh bananas, but frozen will do as well! Drinkers' choice
  3. Milk:Any type of milk will do for this recipe - cow milk, almond milk, oat milk, even breast milk for the little one.
  4. Ice:Now with the ice, you can add as little or as much as you like. Add gradually until satisfied.
  5. Optional Add-Ins
    • Greens
    • Ginger
    • Vanilla
    • Greek yogurt (or any kind of yogurt really)
    • Protein powders
    • Seeds
