Yummy Alfredo Recipe

Description

Rich and creamy Fettuccine Alfredo is one of the easiest and most satisfying pasta dishes to make at home.

Fresh cream is combined with parmesan cheese and butter to make a luscious Alfredo sauce in only minutes.

Toss it with your favorite past for a delicious and hearty vegetarian Italian-esk dinner!

Ingredients

  • For Cooking Pasta

    • half pound Fettuccine
    • six cups water
    • one teaspoon salt (salt to taste)

  • For Sauce

    • three to four tbsp butter
    • half tsp garlic
    • one cup whipping cream
    • sic tbsp parmesan cheese
    • fourth tsp pepper
    • dash grated nutmeg
    • salt
